This is 'Pastel de Papas'

This dish is known in Argentina as potato cake, but in other countries it is called meat cake. It involves layers of mashed potatoes and seasoned ground beef. Cheese is usually added to gratinate it in the oven. It is an easy and cheap recipe.

Here we go, we need only those ingredients:

  • 1 diente de ajo
  • 2 cebollas
  • 1 morrón rojo
  • 2 tomates
  • 1/4 de taza de vino tinto
  • 1 zanahoria
  • 600 gramos de carne picada magra
  • 300 cc de caldo de verduras
  • 1 cucharada de ají molido
  • 1 cucharada de pimentón
  • 1 pizca de sal
  • 1 pizca de pimienta negra
  • 2 hojitas de laurel
  • 1 cucharada de orégano
  • 2 cucharadas de aceite de oliva
  • 6 papas
  • 1 taza de leche
  • 2 cucharadas de manteca

How to do it:

  1. In a large skillet, put the olive oil and the minced garlic. When the garlic begins to brown, add the onion and bell pepper, both cut into cubes.
  2. Let it cook for a few minutes and, as soon as the vegetables start to stick to the pan, deglaze with red wine (add the wine and scrape the pan to raise the cooking bottom).
  3. Let the alcohol evaporate for 2 or 3 minutes and add the tomato without skin and without seeds, cut into cubes, the carrot in very thin slices and the minced meat.
  4. Mix well, breaking up the meat blocks with a wooden spoon, and cook for a few minutes. Season with a little salt, pepper, paprika, ground chili, oregano and bay leaf.
  5. Add broth to cover and cook until the liquid is reduced, stirring so that it does not stick. Add more broth or water if necessary, to cook it for a long time.
  6. Meanwhile, boil the peeled and diced potatoes until they are very tender. Make a puree and add the butter and milk. Season to your taste.
  7. Take the super juicy minced meat to a baking dish and cover with the puree. Gratinate in a strong oven and serve.

Hope you enjoy!
